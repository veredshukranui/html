Benefits of cooking with the kids

Somtimes , just from thinking about it ,I say,"NO THANKS !!
But going throught all the benefits I can esiely chang my mind and say WHY THE HELL NO ???
so here are some of them :

  • The child acquires new skills:cutting,mixing,tasting, kneading, heating etc.
  • you practice fine & gross motor skills and strenghthen them, which is so importent to their development
  • You get to spend more time with your own belovers
  • you succeed taking them out of the screens
  • Your kids get to have fun
  • And in the end... you and your family get to eat from your experienc.. hopfully it will be good & tasty enough

Basic rules before going to the kitchen with them :

  1. For the sack of safety,keep an eye and please make sure that the kids will not touch any dangerous things like knives and barbed wire !
  2. Start your activity with an explenation of who does what- for example ,only a grownup is allowed to light up a fire or touch the oven !
  3. Teach your kids the importance of washing their hands with soap and water befor and after each preperations of food !
